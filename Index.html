Avani Hukkeri

I'm Avani, an aspiring graphic designer from Mumbai with a passion for colors and creativity. I love experimenting with various design mediums and constantly seek new ways to express ideas visually. Exploring different design elements and pushing boundaries fuels my artistic journey, helping me grow as a designer.

Things I enjoy.

Painting.

For me, painting is a way to dive deep into my creativity, much like my love for the sea. Each brushstroke feels like a wave, and I enjoy exploring how colors blend and flow, much like how I approach design. Whether I'm painting ocean scenes or experimenting with abstract ideas, it's a relaxing escape that lets me get lost in the details.

Photography

Photography, on the other hand, is how I capture the beauty and stories that surround me. Through the lens, I can freeze moments that others might miss, framing the world in unique ways. It lets me explore different perspectives, highlight small details, and preserve memories, much like painting with light.

Swimming.

Swimming is my way to feel free and connected to the water, almost like I’m part of the sea itself. Each stroke brings a sense of calm, allowing me to focus and recharge. It’s not just about staying active; it’s a peaceful rhythm that clears my mind and brings balance to my day.
